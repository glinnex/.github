# Welcome to Glinnex 👋

We're an independent cooperative of developers who care deeply about building great software. No investors, no corporate overhead — just a small group of engineers who believe that well-crafted tools make a real difference.

Our work is focused on the developer experience: the tools, workflows, and infrastructure that help teams move faster without cutting corners. We build in the open, ship things we'd want to use ourselves, and try to leave every codebase a little better than we found it.

---

## What we're building

We're currently working on a set of developer tools aimed at reducing the repetitive overhead of starting and maintaining software projects — from initial scaffolding to production-ready infrastructure. Our tools are designed to be modular, opinionated where it matters, and easy to adopt incrementally.

All of our core work is open-source. We believe the best developer tools earn trust by being transparent, and earn revenue by delivering value beyond the tool itself.

---

## How to get involved

We welcome contributions from anyone who shares our passion for quality software. Here's how you can participate:

- **Explore our repositories** — take a look around, open issues, and share feedback
- **Contribute code** — check the `CONTRIBUTING.md` in any of our repos for guidelines
- **Report bugs or suggest features** — we read every issue and take them seriously
- **Spread the word** — if something we've built has helped you, tell someone

If you're interested in collaborating more closely or have ideas you'd like to discuss, feel free to reach out. We're always happy to talk to people who care about the craft.

---

## Our principles

- **Craft-first** — we take the time to do things properly
- **Open by default** — our core tools are free and open-source
- **Developer-native** — we are our own target user
